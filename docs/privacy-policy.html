Privacy Policy — What to Wear

Last updated: June 1, 2026

What to Wear ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal wardrobe organizer app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos: When you scan a clothing item, the photo is sent to Google Gemini API for AI analysis. Photos are not stored on our servers.
  • Wardrobe Data: Items, outfits, and wardrobe data are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not upload this data to any server.
  • Camera Access: Used only when you choose to scan a clothing item.

2. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API — Used for AI clothing analysis. Images are processed by Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — Used to display ads. AdMob may collect device information. See Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage

All wardrobe data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any servers or databases containing your personal information.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
